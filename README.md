Picture this: It's Christmas Eve. You're Santa Claus. But instead of sliding down chimneys with presents, you're **BLASTING PROTEIN-CHUGGING DINOSAURS** who somehow survived extinction by spending 65 MILLION YEARS doing nothing but lifting weights and posting motivational quotes.
